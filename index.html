Rent & Go

Rent & Go application is an application that will be used to make it easier People rent environmentally friendly vehicles (otoped, bicycles, motorcycles, and buggy electrically powered).

Otoped

An otoped or push scooter is a plate with wheels that are usually driven by pushing the ground with the feet. The user stands with one foot on the plate and the other foot is used to drive the otoped.

Motorcycle

Electric motorcycles and scooters are plug-in electric vehicles with two or three wheels. Power is supplied by a rechargeable battery which drives one or more electric motors. can be ridden by 1 or 2 people.

Bicycle

An electric bike is a motorized bicycle with an integrated electric motor used to assist in propulsion. can be ridden for 1 person

Buggy

Buggy Car is a vehicle specially designed for use by visitors with a lot of cargo to travel around the tourist area. This golf cart has 2, 4, 6 to 8 sofas.
